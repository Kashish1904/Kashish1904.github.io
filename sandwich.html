Veg. Grilled Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread (preferred brown) - 4slices
  • Salt - As per requirements
  • Onion - 1
  • Tomato - 1
  • Cucumber - 1/2
  • Capsicum - 1/2
  • Green chilli - 2
  • **You can add more vegetables if want.
  • Schezwan Mayonnaise - 1/2 tsp.
  • Chipotle Sauce - 1/2 tsp.
  • Per-Peri Mayonnaise - 1/2 tsp.
  • Veg Mayonnaise - 1 tsp.
  • Butter/Oil - As required
  • Green Chilli Sauce - 1 tsp.

Procedure

  1. Turn on the griller/toaster to preheat it.
  2. Finely chop all the vegetable taken.
  3. Mix all the chopped vegetables with sauces thoroughly.
  4. Place the prepared mixture and spread uniformly on the bread slice.
  5. Apply butter/oil on preheated griller/toaster.
  6. Cover the prepared slice with other slice of bread and grill on toaster.
  7. Wait until bread becomes crispy and golden.
  8. Take out from the griller/toaster.
  9. Cut the sandwich into two halves and serve hot.
