Fruit Salad Recipe
Yield: 4-5 serves(per recipe)

Tropical Salad

Ingredients
  • Oranges (peeled & halved) - 2
  • Quartered strawberries - 12 ounces
  • Chopped mangoes - 2
  • Kiwis (peeled & chopped/sliced) - 4
For Dressing
  • Lime Juice - 3 Tbsp
  • Maple syrup - Tbsp
Method
  1. Combine all the ingredients above in a large bowl.
  2. Mix the dressing ingredients together and spread over fruit, mix well.
