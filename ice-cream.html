Ice Cream Recipe
Prep time: 15 minutes | Cook time: 10 minutes | Yield: - scoops
Ingredients
  • Milk - 1kg
  • Sugar - 3 Tbsp.
  • Milk Powder - 4 Tsp.
  • Custard Powder - 1 Tsp.
  • Vanilla Essence - 4-5 drops
  • GMS powder - 3-4 Tsp.
  • CMC powder - 1/4 Tsp.
For Dressing

The dressing is easy to make: it’s a zesty mix of olive oil, lemon, garlic, Dijon, herbs & seasonings – I like to use herbes de Provence but Italian seasoning would be equally good in its place.
